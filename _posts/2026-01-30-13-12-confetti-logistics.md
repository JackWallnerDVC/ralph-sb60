## The Hat Runners

You see them every year: the people sprinting onto the field thrusting "CHAMPIONS" caps into the hands of weeping players.

These aren't random staffers. They are "Hat Runners," and they have been drilling their routes since Wednesday. There are 40 of them. Each runner is assigned a specific position group (e.g., Runner 4 has the Offensive Line). They carry exactly 12 hats and 12 t-shirts in a vacuum-sealed bag.

Their goal? To ensure the star Quarterback has a hat on his head before the CBS cameras find him. If you watch closely, you'll see them dodging cameramen like linebackers.

Gone are the days of carrying a wooden platform onto the field. For SB60, the trophy presentation stage is a modular hydraulic unit stored in the North Tunnel.
