# Spec: Reporter Personas & Content Engine

## Goal
Define the editorial voice and persona-driven content generation for the blog.

## Requirements
- **Persona 1: The Insider.** Focused on logistics, stadium news (Levi's Stadium), and "behind the scenes."
- **Persona 2: The Analyst.** Focused on odds, team performance, and data-driven predictions.
- **Persona 3: The Local.** Focused on San Francisco/Santa Clara tourism, food, and fan experience.
- Content must be generated in Markdown with proper front-matter (author, date, tags).

## Definition of Done
- `personas.json` or equivalent defining the three voices.
- First article draft from each persona generated in `content/` or `_posts/`.
