# About SB60 Intel

**SB60 Intel** is your dedicated source for everything Super Bowl 60.

## The Game

Super Bowl 60 takes place on February 8, 2026, at Levi's Stadium in Santa Clara, California. This is the first Super Bowl hosted at Levi's Stadium and the Bay Area's first since Super Bowl 50 in 2016.
