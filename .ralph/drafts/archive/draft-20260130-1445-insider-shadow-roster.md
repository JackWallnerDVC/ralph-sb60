## The Scout Team Reality

This isn't just running routes; this player wears a jersey with the opponent's number and is instructed to mimic their specific release habits and split times.

## The Logistics of Being #54
Life for the Shadow Squad is different this week.
*   **Pay:** They are earning their standard weekly rate (approx. $12,500 for most), not the massive Super Bowl winner's share (which is projected to be over $170,000 per player this year).
*   **Hotels:** While starters have single rooms, practice squad players are often doubled up.
*   **The Call-Up:** They must stay within a 30-minute radius of the stadium until 90 minutes before kickoff. If a starter tweaks a hamstring in warmups, the call comes.

He has been studying the opponent's audible signals for two weeks. If the defense gets a stop on Sunday's final drive, the game ball belongs to the guy who isn't even dressing for the game.
