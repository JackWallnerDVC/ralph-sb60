## Expanded Perimeter: Scope and Impact

The security perimeter around Levi's Stadium will be significantly larger for Super Bowl LX. Sources tell me we're talking about a three-block radius. This expansion will directly affect local businesses and residents. Road closures and increased security checks are to be expected.

Expect closures on Tasman Drive, Great America Parkway, and potentially even Mission College Boulevard. Businesses along these routes are bracing for a hit. Security will include bag checks and metal detectors, not just at the stadium entrances, but at various points within the perimeter.

Understanding the scope of the perimeter is crucial to grasping the logistical challenges and potential disruptions.

## Security Personnel and Costs

A large number of security personnel will be involved in managing the expanded perimeter. We're talking hundreds of officers from Santa Clara PD, the Sheriff's Department, and private security firms. Implementing these security measures comes at a significant cost. The security plan's budget and manpower are substantial.

My sources indicate the number of security personnel could exceed 800. The total cost of implementing the expanded security measures is estimated to be over $2 million. That's a jump of 30% compared to security spending at the last major event held at Levi's.
